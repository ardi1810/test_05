Jobs In Orlando That Pay $20 An Hour

Introduction

Finding a job that pays $20 an hour can be challenging, but not impossible. In Orlando, there are many job opportunities that pay this hourly rate. Whether you're looking for part-time or full-time work, there are options available. In this article, we'll explore some of the jobs in Orlando that pay $20 an hour.

1. Customer Service Representative

One of the most common jobs that pay $20 an hour in Orlando is a customer service representative. These positions are available in a variety of industries, including call centers, retail, and hospitality. Customer service representatives are responsible for answering customer inquiries, resolving issues, and providing excellent service.

2. Licensed Practical Nurse

Another job that pays $20 an hour in Orlando is a licensed practical nurse (LPN). LPNs work in healthcare settings, such as hospitals, clinics, and nursing homes. They provide basic medical care, such as taking vital signs, administering medication, and assisting with patient care.

3. Electrician

Electricians are in high demand in Orlando, and many earn $20 an hour or more. Electricians install and maintain electrical systems, such as wiring, lighting, and circuit breakers. They work in a variety of settings, including residential, commercial, and industrial.

4. Warehouse Worker

A warehouse worker is another job that pays $20 an hour in Orlando. These positions involve working in a warehouse or distribution center, where workers are responsible for receiving, storing, and shipping goods. Warehouse workers may also operate machinery or equipment, such as forklifts, pallet jacks, and conveyor belts.

5. IT Support Specialist

IT support specialists are responsible for providing technical support to computer users. They troubleshoot software and hardware issues, install and maintain computer systems, and provide training to users. Many IT support specialists in Orlando earn $20 an hour or more.

6. Sales Representative

Sales representatives in Orlando can earn $20 an hour or more, depending on their industry and experience. These positions involve selling products or services to customers, either in person or over the phone. Sales representatives may also be responsible for generating leads, following up with customers, and meeting sales quotas.

7. Graphic Designer

If you have a creative eye and love design, a graphic designer job in Orlando may be a great fit for you. Many graphic designers earn $20 an hour or more. They create visual designs for a variety of mediums, including websites, logos, advertisements, and product packaging.

8. Personal Trainer

If you're passionate about fitness and helping others, a personal trainer job in Orlando may be a great fit. Many personal trainers earn $20 an hour or more, and they work in gyms, health clubs, and other fitness facilities. They design exercise plans, provide instruction and motivation, and track progress for their clients.

9. Web Developer

Web developers are in high demand in Orlando, and many earn $20 an hour or more. They design and develop websites, ensuring that they are functional, user-friendly, and visually appealing. Web developers may also be responsible for maintaining and updating websites, as well as troubleshooting issues.

10. HVAC Technician

If you enjoy working with your hands and have a mechanical aptitude, an HVAC technician job in Orlando may be a great fit. HVAC technicians install and maintain heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems in residential and commercial settings. Many HVAC technicians earn $20 an hour or more.

Conclusion

Whether you're looking for a career change or just starting out in the workforce, there are many jobs in Orlando that pay $20 an hour or more. From customer service to healthcare, from IT to fitness, there are options available for everyone. Use this article as a starting point to explore your options and find the job that's right for you.
