The Legend of Steve Jobs

Steve Jobs was a technology entrepreneur, designer and inventor who co-founded Apple Inc. in 1976, with Steve Wozniak. He was the man behind the success of Apple, and his vision revolutionized the world of personal computing, music, phones, and tablet devices. Even after his death in 2011, he remains an inspiration to many.
