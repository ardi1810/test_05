Part-Time Job Opportunities at Amazon

Amazon offers a range of part-time job opportunities in Tulsa. These include positions in fulfillment centers, sortation centers, and delivery stations. Each of these facilities requires a different set of skills and offers different benefits.

Fulfillment Center Jobs

At Amazon's fulfillment centers in Tulsa, part-time workers can work in a variety of roles, including packing, shipping, and receiving. Sortation Center Jobs

Sortation centers in Tulsa offer part-time workers the opportunity to work in a fast-paced environment, sorting packages by zip code for delivery. Part-time workers at sortation centers typically work 15-20 hours per week and receive benefits such as dental and vision insurance.

Delivery Station Jobs

Delivery stations are where packages are loaded onto delivery vehicles for final delivery. Part-time workers at delivery stations in Tulsa typically work 10-15 hours per week and receive benefits such as flexible schedules and paid time off.
