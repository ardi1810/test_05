Introduction

Are you a student looking for a part-time job in Bangalore after 5 pm? Or are you a working professional who wants to earn some extra money in their free time? Whatever your situation may be, this article will guide you through some of the best part-time jobs in Bangalore that you can do after 5 pm.

1. Food Delivery Jobs

One of the most popular part-time jobs in Bangalore after 5 pm is food delivery. With the rise of food delivery apps like Swiggy, Zomato, and Uber Eats, there is a high demand for delivery executives. All you need is a two-wheeler and a smartphone, and you can earn up to Rs. 25,000 per month.

2. Data Entry Jobs

Data entry jobs are another popular option for part-time work in Bangalore after 5 pm. Many companies hire data entry operators to input data into their systems. These jobs are ideal for students or anyone who can type quickly and accurately. You can earn up to Rs. 15,000 per month doing data entry work.

3. Content Writing Jobs

If you have a flair for writing, you can consider taking up content writing jobs. Many companies hire freelance writers to create content for their websites, blogs, and social media. You can earn up to Rs. 30,000 per month as a content writer.

4. Virtual Assistant Jobs

Virtual assistant jobs are ideal for those who are good at multitasking and have excellent organizational skills. As a virtual assistant, you will be responsible for tasks like scheduling appointments, managing emails, and making travel arrangements. You can earn up to Rs. 25,000 per month as a virtual assistant.

5. Tutoring Jobs

If you have a passion for teaching, you can consider taking up tutoring jobs after 5 pm. You can either work with a tutoring company or offer your services as a private tutor. You can earn up to Rs. 20,000 per month as a tutor.

6. Event Management Jobs

Event management jobs are ideal for those who are good at planning and organizing events. You can work with an event management company or offer your services as a freelancer. You can earn up to Rs. 30,000 per month as an event manager.

7. Social Media Management Jobs

Social media management jobs are ideal for those who are good at using social media platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter. Many companies hire social media managers to create and manage their social media accounts. You can earn up to Rs. 25,000 per month as a social media manager.

8. Photography Jobs

If you have a passion for photography, you can consider taking up photography jobs after 5 pm. You can work with a photography company or offer your services as a freelancer. You can earn up to Rs. 20,000 per month as a photographer.

9. Graphic Design Jobs

Graphic design jobs are ideal for those who are good at using design software like Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. Many companies hire graphic designers to create logos, brochures, and other marketing materials. You can earn up to Rs. 30,000 per month as a graphic designer.

10. Customer Service Jobs

Customer service jobs are ideal for those who are good at communicating with people. Many companies hire customer service representatives to handle customer queries and complaints. You can earn up to Rs. 20,000 per month as a customer service representative.

Conclusion

In conclusion, there are plenty of part-time job opportunities available in Bangalore after 5 pm. You can choose from a variety of options depending on your skills and interests. These part-time jobs can provide you with a steady source of income while allowing you to pursue your studies or other interests. So, go ahead and explore the various part-time job opportunities in Bangalore after 5 pm and find one that suits you best!
