Introduction

The demand for driver jobs in Chennai has been increasing over the years due to the growth in the transportation sector. This has led to a rise in the number of companies offering driver jobs in Chennai, making it easier for job seekers to find employment in this field. If you are looking for a driver job in Chennai, here are some tips and contact numbers you can use to find the right job for you.

Job Requirements

To qualify for a driver job in Chennai, you must have a valid driving license and a minimum of 2 years of driving experience. You should also be familiar with the traffic rules and regulations in Chennai. Most companies require drivers to have a high school diploma or equivalent. Good communication skills in English or Tamil are also necessary, as you will be interacting with customers and colleagues.

Job Search Tips

To find driver jobs in Chennai, you can use various online job portals such as Naukri, Monster, and Indeed. You can also contact driver recruitment agencies such as Driver Placement Services, which specializes in providing driver jobs in Chennai. Another option is to visit transport companies and inquire about job vacancies directly.

Job Responsibilities

As a driver, your main responsibility is to transport passengers or goods to their destination safely and on time. You should also ensure that the vehicle is well-maintained and in good condition. Other responsibilities may include keeping records of trips, collecting payments, and reporting any accidents or incidents to the authorities.

Salary and Benefits

The salary for driver jobs in Chennai varies depending on the company and the type of vehicle you are driving. On average, drivers can earn between Rs. 15,000 and Rs. 25,000 per month. Some companies also offer benefits such as health insurance, paid vacation, and retirement plans.

Training and Certification

Some companies may provide on-the-job training to new drivers. However, if you want to improve your skills and increase your chances of getting hired, you can enroll in a driving school or training program. You can also obtain additional certifications such as first aid and defensive driving to enhance your resume.

Conclusion

Driver jobs in Chennai offer a great opportunity for those who enjoy driving and want to make a career in the transportation sector. By following the tips and contact numbers mentioned in this article, you can find the right job for you and start your journey towards a successful career as a driver.
