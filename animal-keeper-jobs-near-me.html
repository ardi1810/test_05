Introduction

Looking for a career that involves working with animals? Consider becoming an animal keeper! Animal keepers are responsible for the daily care and well-being of animals in zoos, aquariums, wildlife parks, and other animal-related facilities. In this article, we will discuss various animal keeper jobs available near you in 2023.

Qualifications

To become an animal keeper, you must possess a deep love and respect for animals. High school diploma or equivalent is required, and a degree in animal science, biology, or zoology is preferred. Relevant experience working with animals is also an advantage.

Job Duties

Animal keepers are responsible for ensuring the animals' physical and emotional well-being. They feed and water the animals, clean and maintain their habitats, and provide environmental enrichment activities to keep them mentally stimulated. They also observe and report any changes in the animals' behavior, health, or welfare.

Types of Animal Keeper Jobs

There are many types of animal keeper jobs available, including:

Zookeeper

Zookeepers work in zoological parks and maintain the daily care and welfare of animals. They also provide educational programs to visitors and the community.

Aquarist

Aquarists work in aquariums and are responsible for maintaining the aquatic animals' health and habitats.

Wildlife Keeper

Wildlife keepers work in wildlife parks and sanctuaries and care for animals that are native to the area.

Salary

The salary for animal keepers varies depending on the facility and location. The average salary for an animal keeper in the United States is $29,000 to $50,000 per year.

Job Outlook

According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, employment of animal care and service workers is projected to grow 16% from 2020 to 2030, much faster than the average for all occupations. The demand for animal keeper jobs is expected to increase due to the growing interest in animal welfare and the conservation of endangered species.

Conclusion

If you have a passion for animals and want to work with them, becoming an animal keeper is a great career choice. There are many different types of animal keeper jobs available near you in 2023, and the demand for these jobs is expected to grow. Start your journey today by researching the animal keeper jobs available in your area and taking the necessary steps to pursue this rewarding career.
