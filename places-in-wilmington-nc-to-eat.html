Discover The Best Places To Eat In Wilmington, Nc

Introduction

If you're visiting Wilmington, NC, or you're a local looking for new dining experiences, you're in luck. This charming city has a diverse culinary scene that caters to every taste bud. From seafood shacks to upscale eateries, you'll find something that suits your appetite. In this article, we'll highlight ten places you must try when in Wilmington.

The Basics

Before we dive into the list, it's essential to know a few things. Wilmington is a coastal city, so seafood is a staple on most menus. However, you'll find plenty of vegetarian and vegan options. Also, it's always wise to make reservations, especially during peak tourist seasons.

1. The Basics

The Basics is a trendy breakfast spot that serves up classic dishes with a modern twist. Their menu features southern favorites like biscuits and gravy and chicken and waffles. The restaurant has a cozy outdoor seating area, perfect for enjoying your morning coffee.

2. Rx Restaurant and Bar

Rx Restaurant and Bar is a farm-to-table eatery that's committed to using locally sourced ingredients. They offer an extensive wine list and craft cocktails. The menu changes often, depending on what's in season, but their fried chicken sandwich is a must-try.

3. Ceviche's

Located in the heart of downtown, Ceviche's is a Peruvian restaurant that serves up fresh seafood dishes. Their ceviche is a fan favorite, but don't overlook their lomo saltado or arroz con mariscos. The restaurant has a cozy atmosphere, perfect for a date night.

4. Tower 7 Baja Mexican Grill

Craving some Mexican cuisine? Tower 7 Baja Mexican Grill has you covered. Their menu features fresh ingredients and bold flavors. Their fish tacos and margaritas are highly recommended. The restaurant has a laid-back vibe, perfect for a casual dinner.

5. Elijah's Restaurant

Elijah's Restaurant is a seafood lover's paradise. The restaurant overlooks the Cape Fear River, providing stunning views while you dine. Their seafood platter is a must-try, featuring a variety of fresh catches. Don't forget to try their oysters.

6. PinPoint Restaurant

PinPoint Restaurant is a fine dining establishment that offers a farm-to-table experience. Their menu features classic southern dishes with a modern twist. Their shrimp and grits and fried green tomatoes are highly recommended. The restaurant has an elegant atmosphere, perfect for a special occasion.

7. Roko Italian Cuisine

Craving Italian food? Roko Italian Cuisine is the place to go. Their menu features classic Italian dishes with a modern twist. Their homemade pasta is a must-try, and their wine list is extensive. The restaurant has a cozy atmosphere, perfect for a romantic dinner.

8. Paddy's Hollow Restaurant and Pub

Paddy's Hollow Restaurant and Pub is an Irish pub that serves up hearty comfort food. Their shepherd's pie and fish and chips are highly recommended. The restaurant has a casual atmosphere, perfect for a night out with friends.

9. Indochine Restaurant

Indochine Restaurant is a Vietnamese restaurant that offers a unique dining experience. Their menu features traditional Vietnamese dishes with a modern twist. Their pho and summer rolls are must-tries. The restaurant has a lively atmosphere, perfect for a group dinner.

10. Blue Surf Cafe

Blue Surf Cafe is a laid-back breakfast and lunch spot that serves up fresh and healthy dishes. Their menu features vegetarian and vegan options, as well as gluten-free items. Their avocado toast and acai bowls are highly recommended. The cafe has a relaxed atmosphere, perfect for a casual meal.

Conclusion

Wilmington, NC, is a foodie's paradise, and these ten restaurants are just the tip of the iceberg. Whether you're in the mood for seafood, Mexican, Italian, or Vietnamese cuisine, you'll find something that suits your taste buds. So, what are you waiting for? Grab your fork and start exploring the culinary scene in Wilmington.
