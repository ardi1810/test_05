Introduction

Vancouver, WA is a beautiful city located in the Pacific Northwest. With its scenic beauty, excellent schools, and low crime rates, it has become a popular destination for families looking for a great place to live. In this article, we will take a closer look at some of the best neighborhoods in Vancouver, WA.

Downtown Vancouver

Downtown Vancouver is a vibrant and bustling neighborhood that offers a mix of modern amenities and historic charm. The neighborhood is home to a variety of shops, restaurants, and entertainment venues, making it an ideal place for young professionals or those who love the city life. The neighborhood is also home to some of the city's most iconic landmarks, including Esther Short Park, which hosts a variety of events throughout the year.

Salmon Creek

Salmon Creek is a quiet, suburban neighborhood that is perfect for families. The neighborhood is home to some of the best schools in the area, including Salmon Creek Elementary, Alki Middle School, and Skyview High School. In addition to its excellent schools, Salmon Creek is also home to a variety of parks and recreational areas, making it an ideal place for outdoor enthusiasts.

Camas

Camas is a picturesque neighborhood located just a few miles east of Vancouver. The neighborhood is known for its stunning views of the Columbia River Gorge and its small-town charm. Camas is home to a variety of shops and restaurants, as well as some of the best schools in the area. The neighborhood is also home to Lacamas Lake, which offers a variety of outdoor activities, including fishing, boating, and hiking.

Orchards

Orchards is a diverse neighborhood located in the heart of Vancouver. The neighborhood is home to a variety of shops, restaurants, and entertainment venues, making it an ideal place for young professionals. Hazel Dell

Hazel Dell is a peaceful, suburban neighborhood located just north of Vancouver. The neighborhood is known for its tree-lined streets and well-manicured lawns. Vancouver, WA is a great place to live, with a variety of neighborhoods that offer something for everyone. Whether you're looking for a bustling city center or a peaceful suburban retreat, Vancouver has it all. So why not consider making Vancouver, WA your home?
