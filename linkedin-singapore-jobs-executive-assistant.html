Introduction

LinkedIn is a popular professional networking site that connects people all over the world. It is also a great platform for job seekers and employers to find each other. One of the most popular job categories on LinkedIn is executive assistant jobs. In this article, we will discuss some of the best executive assistant jobs available in Singapore.

What is an Executive Assistant?

An executive assistant is a professional who provides administrative support to a company's top-level executives. They are responsible for handling confidential information, managing schedules, arranging meetings, and handling other administrative tasks. They need to have excellent communication and organizational skills.

Top Executive Assistant Jobs in Singapore

1. Executive Assistant at DBS Bank

DBS Bank is a leading bank in Singapore and is currently looking for an executive assistant to provide support to their senior management team. The ideal candidate should have at least 5 years of experience as an executive assistant, excellent communication skills, and be able to work under pressure.

2. Executive Assistant at Google

Google is one of the most popular tech companies in the world and is currently looking for an executive assistant to support their senior executives in Singapore. The ideal candidate should have at least 3 years of experience as an executive assistant, be able to multitask, and have excellent communication skills.

3. Executive Assistant at Amazon

Amazon is a leading e-commerce company and is currently looking for an executive assistant to support their senior executives in Singapore. The ideal candidate should have at least 5 years of experience as an executive assistant, excellent organizational skills, and be able to work in a fast-paced environment.

Skills Required for an Executive Assistant Job

To be successful as an executive assistant, you need to have the following skills:

1. Communication Skills

You need to have excellent communication skills, both verbal and written, to effectively communicate with senior executives.

2. Organizational Skills

You need to have excellent organizational skills to manage schedules, arrange meetings, and handle other administrative tasks.

3. Time Management Skills

You need to have excellent time management skills to prioritize tasks and meet deadlines.

4. Attention to Detail

You need to have excellent attention to detail to ensure that all tasks are completed accurately and efficiently.

Conclusion

If you are looking for an executive assistant job in Singapore, LinkedIn is a great platform to start your job search. There are many great companies that are currently looking for executive assistants, and with the right skills and experience, you can land your dream job. Just remember to highlight your communication, organizational, time management, and attention to detail skills in your job application.
